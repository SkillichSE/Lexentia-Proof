Methods — Scoring

Normalization

Each metric is normalized 0–100 within its size tier. A Small model is only compared to other Small models, preventing unfair comparisons across parameter counts.

score = (raw − min) / (max − min) × 100

Quality Formula

The final quality score is a weighted average of the four benchmark scores, with code and reasoning weighted higher due to their objective nature.

Q = 0.30·HE + 0.30·GSM + 0.25·MMLU + 0.15·TR
